About the Festival

Paris will vibrate on June 21 to celebrate the Fête de la Musique. From jazz in intimate clubs to pop in open-air spaces, explore the city's best music events with us!

Event Highlights

Vibrations Jazz au Duc des Lombards

Artist: Ibrahim Maalouf

Genre: Jazz Fusion

Time: 18:00

Location: Duc des Lombards, Rue des Lombards

Details: Experience the unique sounds of Ibrahim Maalouf, a talented trumpet player blending jazz and Arabic music, in the intimate setting of Duc des Lombards.

Soirée Pop à Place de la République

Artist: Angèle

Genre: Pop

Time: 20:00

Location: Place de la République

Details: Belgian pop sensation Angèle will perform her hits at one of the most iconic public spaces in Paris, combining music with the beauty of the cityscape.

Indie Rock au Trianon

Artist: Phoenix

Genre: Indie Rock

Time: 21:00

Location: Le Trianon, Boulevard Rochechouart

Details: Join the legendary indie rock band Phoenix for an unforgettable performance at the historic Trianon Theatre, one of Paris' most renowned venues.

Electro à Parc de la Villette

Artist: Vitalic

Genre: Electro

Time: 22:00

Location: Parc de la Villette

Details: Feel the beats at Parc de la Villette, where Vitalic's electrifying performance will light up the night with energetic electronic music.

Légendes du Rock au Bataclan

Artist: Indochine

Genre: Rock

Time: 19:00

Location: Bataclan, Boulevard Voltaire

Details: Indochine brings their legendary rock sound to the iconic Bataclan venue. Don't miss this electrifying night of classic French rock.

Soirée Hip-Hop Français à La Cigale

Artist: Lomepal

Genre: Hip-Hop

Time: 21:30

Location: La Cigale, Boulevard de Rochechouart

Details: Lomepal’s rap-infused beats combined with heartfelt lyrics promise an unforgettable night at La Cigale, one of Paris' best music venues.

Chef-d'œuvre Classique au Théâtre des Champs-Élysées

Artist: Orchestre National de France

Genre: Classical Music

Time: 19:30

Location: Théâtre des Champs-Élysées, Avenue Montaigne

Details: Enjoy the elegance of classical music performed by the Orchestre National de France at the stunning Théâtre des Champs-Élysées.

Soirée Folk au Café de la Danse

Artist: Pomme

Genre: Folk/Chanson Française

Time: 20:00

Location: Café de la Danse, Rue de Lappe

Details: Pomme’s poetic folk music creates an intimate atmosphere, making this performance at Café de la Danse one to remember.

Événement de Clôture - Feu d'artifice et DJ Set à la Tour Eiffel

Artist: DJ Snake

Genre: EDM

Time: 23:30

Location: Tour Eiffel, Champ de Mars

Details: End the night with a spectacular fireworks display and an electrifying DJ set by DJ Snake at the iconic Eiffel Tower.
